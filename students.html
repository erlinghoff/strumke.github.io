For students

In my opinion, students are the blood and life of academia. If you are a master student at the NTNU or SimulaMet, please find descriptions of the projects I offer on the institutions' web sites. The best projects often come out of students' own ideas, so if you have an idea you would like to discuss with me, get in touch and we'll find time for a meeting.

If you are a PhD student interested in XAI, you can get in touch with me to discuss project ideas regardless of affiliation.
